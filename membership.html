FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Can I cancel my membership anytime?

Monthly memberships can be cancelled with 30 days notice. Annual memberships require 60 days notice but offer significant savings. Premium memberships are flexible with 30 days notice.

Is there a joining fee?

We currently have a promotional offer with no joining fees for new members. Standard joining fee is $50 when not on promotion.

Can I freeze my membership?

Yes, you can freeze your membership for up to 3 months per year for a small monthly fee of $10. This is perfect for vacations or temporary circumstances.

Do you offer student or military discounts?

Absolutely! We offer 20% off monthly memberships for students with valid ID and 25% off for active military personnel and veterans.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, bank transfers, and digital payment methods like PayPal and Apple Pay.
